Who we are?

We started our journey back in 1991, to provide PERFECT solutions to our clients by following an innovative approach.


We operate from the capital city of New Delhi having approx 7200 sq ft. office space with dedicated work stations for our team members.
We take pride in having recruited and developed a strong work force of over 160 talented, energetic & young leaders (chartered accountants, company secretaries, experienced accountants, insolvency professionals, registered valuers, independent directors, MBA's, law graduates & project specialists) with a renowned and strong leadership team having extensive knowledge and a great deal of expertise.

  • Obtaining prior approval/ registration to accept foreign contribution as required under Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act (FCRA)
  • Seeking post facto approval/ regularization of foreign contribution received in violation of FCRA regulations
  • Filling of annual returns and other compliance on behalf of society/ trust accepting foreign contribution

    • Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) approvals


  • Obtaining approvals and exemptions under various provisions of Companies Act 2013

    • Facilitating foreign investment


  • Obtaining approval from respective ministries to undertake activities not covered under automatic investment route
  • Obtaining approval from respective ministries for transfer of shares of Indian companies from resident Indians to non-resident Indians, and vice versa for sectors not covered under automatic route
  • Seeking clarifications on interpretation/ applicability of FEMA provisions from Department of Industrial Policy and Promotion (DIPP)

    • Foreign Exchange Management Act, 1999 (FEMA) approvals


  • Preparing and filing of form FC-GPR pursuant to issue of shares to a non-resident investor
  • Preparing and filing of annual return on foreign liabilities and assets with Reserve Bank of India
  • Compounding of offence on contravention of FEMA regulations
  • Obtaining approval/ registration for accepting external commercial borrowings
  • Obtaining approval for extension of operation of liaison office established by a foreign entity in India
  • Obtaining various approvals under FEMA act, rules, regulations, notifications, circulars, press notes etc.
  • Seeking case specific clarifications from RBI regional/ central office
  • Advising and facilitating investment under ODI, portfolio and LRS route
  • Advising/ representing on matters relating to Enforcement Directorate

    • Companies Act, 2013 approvals


  • Shifting of registered office from one state to another
  • Seeking approval for following a financial year ending other than March 31 every year
  • Representing/ seeking clearances from ROC/ RD/ OL/ NCLT on matters of restructuring (merger/ demerger/ buy back)
  • Compounding of offences under Companies Act, 2013
  • Obtaining approvals and exemptions under various provisions of Companies Act, 2013
  • Seeking extension of time for holding the AGM from ROC
  • Assisting in conversion of firm/ company into LLP or private to public company etc.
  • Assisting in obtaining approvals related to CSR
